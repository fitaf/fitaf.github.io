Effects of Corona

  1. Somebody fills out the invoice templates
  2. This somebody prints the invoice
  3. Gives the invoice to accounting!!!
  4. Accounting does book keeping
  5. Give the invoice to postal guy!!!
  7. You take the invoice!!!
  8. Put invoice in an envelope
  9. Bring envelope into a post office (& cue up!!!)
  10. Buy stamp & send away!!!
  11. go to the office
  12. (1 day later) the other side receives your invoice & unpacks!!!
  13. The other side does accounting!!!
  14. Invoice payment is setup
  15. Invoice is archived by the archive guy!!!

Summary: 1h each side gone & 7 x !!! & waiting time for payment

CARAMELOW

Just imagine you have an invoicing app. For sake of simpicity lets call it CARAMELOW. You have a 2-sided market with a) suppliers and b) merchants.
Because invoicing is quite complex and you do technical stuff for both sides (e.g. a marketplace in the web) hence you all decided that you will generate the invoicesyou generate invoices for both sides you have agreed.
In case of a supplier (the [invoice-giver]) you are the [invoice- taker]. Vice Versa for Merchants.
Every month you conduct invoicing for both sides & are sending eMails to respective parties.
Process steps today without FITAF:

  1. Open CARAMELOW
  2. Log into CARAMELOW (could be skipped with endurable web log-ins, mostly Cookie based)
  3. Download the invoice
  4. Open your browser
  5. Open your web-mailer
  6. Create new email
  7. Put recipient in to field
  8. Put your tax consultant in CC field
  9. Write text
  10. Attach File
  11. Press "Send"
  12. Imagine your E-Mail is received immeaditely (which practically is never the case!!!!)
  13. Customer forwards to his tax consultant>/li>
  14. Your tax consultant reads your e-Mails
  15. ...and does his bookings in accouning
  16. The taker's tax cunsultant also procceses the attachments
  17. The head of payments sets up a payment for you with the right value
  18. You check the height & execute the payment

You decide to call the "FITAF invoiving API" via a schedule job. By this CARAMELO triggerers automated send-outs and conducts automated book keeping on your side (in theory both sides). (IT Development Effort = approx. 1 day with testing)
