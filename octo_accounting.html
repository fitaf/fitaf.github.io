Octo Accounting - Definition



  1. Singular accounting
    Capturing of all in- & outbound payments
  2. Double accounting accounting
    ...and bills
  3. Triple accounting
    Payments for the receiving company too
  4. Quadruple accounting
    In- & outbound payments & invoices for both parties (giver & taker)
  5. Octo Accounting
    Actually this should word should be more precise :-) (8=2x2x2)
