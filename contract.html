Input into the contract

  • "Value"- (See below instructions below.). The last 10 companies are double checked, so no cheating here :-). Actually we trust you to fill it out correctly. When there is time we will double check!
  • Company Name (& Company Type) - e.g. FahrtenFuchs GmbH
  • Company Address (the postal adddress & the E-Mail)
  • Managing Director of the company signing the contract
  • Signature of Managing Director (or sub-ordinate)
  • Telephone number(needed for calling you in case of questions!)


"Value" (FITAF Business Model)

50% of cost of last years cost for accounting, company tax declarations, yearly balances. Divided by twelve to get a monthly figure. Dont forget to add the internal hours spent collecting invoices & triggering payouts. (The yearly costs are divided by 12 to get a monthly figure,which is charged monthly at the beginning of every month.



The process of gigning up

  1. Download contract
  2. Fill ot the "input" (see above)
  3. Get internal signature (mostly from MD or person who got the power to sign (e.g. CFO))
  4. Send contract as an attachment to an E-Mail to...(see contact)
  5. We are reconfirming by setting up an account for the respective company
  6. In case of questions, we will call you on the phone number mentioned in the contract!!!

You are charged from the time we set up the account for you. Best set-up a recurring payment transfer. (At start we do not atotomatically put FITAF charges as a cost to your companies' accountant!!!)
