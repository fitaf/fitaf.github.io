FITAF (Fury In The Accounting Factory)

Vision/Advantages/Features

  • Go into the shop and say: “Payment with 258" (ID of company) - No manual payment & no manual book keeping - best case: both sides
  • Own way of payment for immediate processing within the FITAF System
  • Automatic payments & book keeping
  • Ensuring and processing of payments in real-time(also on weekends!) [“FITAF-Debit”]
  • Suggestions for beek keeping accounts based on:
    1. Bookings in the past
    2. The invoice giver or taker(=the other party on the invoice)
    3. Other companies (3rd parties)
  • Reporting aiming for managers (not book keepers)
  • Payment clearing among all parties
  • international payment clearing
