FITAF (Fury In The Accounting Factory)

Vision/Advantages/Features

  • Go into a shop and after selectin goods, say to the cashier: “Payment with FITAF 258" (ID of company) -> No manual payment & no manual book keeping - best case: both sides
  • FITAF "way of payment" for immediate processing within the FITAF System -> no classical money movement B2B
  • Automatic payments & book keeping
  • Ensuring and processing of payments in "real-time" (also on weekends!) [“FITAF-Debit”]
  • Suggestions for book keeping accounts based on:
    1. Bookings in the past
    2. The invoice giver or invoice taker (=the other party on the invoice | bascially the company (giber/taker) providing the invoice)
    3. Other companies (3rd parties)
  • Reporting aiming for managers (not book keepers)
  • Payment clearing among all parties -> no classical money movement B2B
  • International payment clearing across currencies -> no classical money movement B2B
