Quadruple Accounting - Definition



  1. Singular accounting
    Capturing of all in- & outbound payments

  2. Double accounting accounting
    ...and bills

  3. Triple accounting
    Payments for the receiving company too

  4. Quadruple accounting
    In- & outbound payments & invoices for both parties (giver & taker)

Definition Invoices - Giver Invoices - Receiver Payments - Giver Payments - Receiver
1x Accounting (payments of giver) No No Yes No
2x Accounting (tackles only givers) Yes No Yes No
3x Accounting (theoretical - nobody implemeents this) Yes No Yes Yes
4x Accounting - full fledged handling for both sides Yes Yes Yes Yes
